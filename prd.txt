1. System Architecture: The Triad
Eco-Sync has evolved from a simple reporting app into a full-scale Enterprise
Resource Planning (ERP) platform. The ecosystem is divided into three distinct
operational layers that interact in real-time:
1. The User
The paying customer
generating demand. Focuses
on billing, bin requests, and
triggering pickup alerts.
2. The Admin
The strategic command.
Focuses on HR, route
generation, fleet
management, and financial
oversight.
3. The Staff
The execution layer on the
ground. Divided into Team
Leads (Nav/Verification) and
Normal Staff (Rosters).

3. Advanced Logic & Enterprise Protocols
To ensure business viability and operational truth, Eco-Sync relies on three advanced
logic gates:
A. The Route-Health Algorithm (Auto-Scaling)
Admins do not manually hunt for full bins. The system groups houses by 
route_id. Once 70% of users on "Route Alpha" trigger the "Bin Full" alarm, the
system automatically elevates the route to CRITICAL status and recommends
dispatch.
B. QR/NFC Proof of Service (Anti-Fraud)
Staff cannot blindly click "Resolved" from the truck. Every physical bin contains a
registered QR code. The Team Lead must scan the QR code to clear the bin from
the map, triggering an automatic SMS/Push to the User confirming the exact
pickup time.
C. Defaulter Logic (Revenue Protection)
If a User fails to pay their monthly fee:
1. Their app access is restricted to the Payment Gateway.
